8 Factors, Scored and Explained

Every audit covers the dimensions that determine whether visitors become customers.

First Impression
Does the page communicate value within 3 seconds? Will visitors stay or immediately bounce?
Value Proposition
Is it clear what you offer, who it’s for, and why it’s the right choice?
Call to Action
Are CTAs visible, compelling, and positioned where intent is highest?
Trust Signals
Testimonials, logos, certifications — the elements that reduce purchase anxiety.
Mobile Experience
60%+ of traffic is mobile. Is every action easy to complete on a small screen?
Page Speed
Every 1s of delay costs ~7% in conversions. Speed is a conversion lever.
Copy Clarity
Is the language specific, benefit-driven, and written for the right audience?
Visual Hierarchy
Does the layout guide attention toward conversion, or create cognitive overload?
